The Legend of Huarong Path

This ancient Chinese sliding block puzzle is based on a famous historical event from the Three Kingdoms period. The game represents Cao Cao's escape after his defeat at the Battle of Red Cliffs.

Your goal is to help Cao Cao (the large red block) reach the exit at the bottom. Move the blocks by clicking and dragging them through the narrow path.
